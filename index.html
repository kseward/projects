Old School Hawaii


In partnership with Kali Navales and the Kumuola foundation

All my life I have felt out of place in the modern world, disconnected from nature. As I grew older, my anxiety from this superficial civilization grew, and I knew I had to make a change. I prayed to Akua deep in the valleys, asking for guidance. With a great gust of wind I received an answer: Bring back the roots to the world. Bring the people together. Spread aloha from keiki to kupuna. Plant forests of fruit trees and miles of lo'i kalo.

Bringing together elegant and catchy guitar picking, exciting melodies, and beautiful harmonies, this song aims to spread awareness and hope about our way of life. Old School Hawaii is used as the theme song for tv show Cool For Kupuna. Find it on all streaming platforms.
